Features

How It Works

Simple, beautiful, and completely browser-based

Real-Time Audio

Powered by Web Audio API for authentic piano sound synthesis. Each note is generated in real-time for a genuine musical experience.

Visual Piano

Watch the piano keys light up as each song plays. A beautiful visualization that brings the music to life on your screen.

Keyboard Play

Play the piano yourself using your computer keyboard. Keys Z-M for lower octave, Q-P for upper octave. Space to play/pause.

Fully Responsive

Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Take the Christmas spirit with you wherever you go.

No Installation

Runs entirely in your browser. No downloads, no plugins, no hassle. Just open and enjoy the music.

Speed Control

Adjust playback speed to your preference. Slow it down to learn the melody or speed it up for variety.
